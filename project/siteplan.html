Site Name: Aspen Summit Resort

  • This name shows that it is located in Aspen Colorado and also explains that it is a resort in the mountains for skiing/snowboarding.

Site Purpose: The site allows people to see information about the ski resort, what the resort looks like, how to book tickets, how to purchase season passes, and how to contact the resort.

Scenarios:

  • What do the trials look like in the mountains?
  • How much are season passes?
  • How much are lift tickets?
  • How can I contact the ski resort?

Color Schema:

  • Light blue: ACD9EC, used as background color for buttons when purchasing lift tickets.
  • Charcoal: 374349, used as color background for the footer section, the font color for paragraph elements, and also as background color for any other buttons besides the "purchase lift tickets" button.
  • Anti-flash white: E8ECED, used as background color for most of the article elements.
  • White: Used as primary background for the entire main sections and most of the body and occasionally the font color for smaller headings.
  • Yale blue: 003B7A, Used as the background color for the header elements and bigger headings within the body.

Font: Oswald

  • This font will be used throughout the entire site, however it will be in all caps and semi-bold when used in the headings.
